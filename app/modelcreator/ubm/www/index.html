Getting Started

  • Write a Review Point
    or Open Item

  • Each Item on every Table of Contents has two links, the reference link and the item link.
    The Reference Link will take you to that section of the UBM Reference Manual
    The Item Link will take you to that section of the UBM Suite.
    In your Universal Business Model Creation Suite package you will see folders for the four major sections.

    (I) Setup,
    (II) Model Creator,
    (III) App Store
    (IV) External Developers Section.
    (V) Internal Developers Section.

    You will also see a table of contents after the folders. Open the UBM Suite Table of Contents and review. You will notice the four major sections are listed again on the table of contents.

  • The easiest way to begin is to start on the company (do not print yet). All of the table of contents and checklists have been linked to all the forms you need to complete.
    In your Universal Business Model Creation Suite package you will see folders for the four major sections.

    (A) Model Creation,
    (B) Source Model,
    (C) Executable Model, and
    (D) Operating Model.

    You will also see a table of contents after the folders. Open the Table of Contents and review. You will notice the four major sections are listed again on the table of contents. Under the (A) Model Creation section you will see a line referenced AMC. This is the checklist you will need to complete to have a functioning model. Click the AMC Model Creation Suite Phases Checklist. Each step on the checklist has been linked to the correct document you need to complete to check off the step. When you complete a step you need to initial and date the prepared by columns (located to the left of the steps).

  • Keep Track of your time for each step when creating your model (use the columns to the right of each step on the checklist.

  • If you choose to start out by keeping hard copies of each form you can print off the pre-defined binder tabs located under the Table of contents file and create a binder using those. You do not have to create a binder at the first because the checklist will walk you through the steps eventually to do this. The only reason you would create the binder before the checklist says to, is if your manager needs a manual copy and needs to review each form individually.

  • All creation forms have already been referenced correctly you are not supposed to change any reference on any pre-defined form. All you need to do is add your company name in the center header of all forms.

  • Finally the UBM Creation Suite has a reference manual included in every version. The Reference Manual includes the explanation, templates, and samples you can refer to too assist you in completing your model forms.

  • If needed review the referencing Protocol and Referencing guide located in the Reference Manual in the following folder trail:
    (B) Model Source > 01 Reference Manual > 00 Gen > 03 Ref Pro
